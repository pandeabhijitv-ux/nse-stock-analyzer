Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

NSE Stock Analyzer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.

Our app:

  • Does NOT require user accounts or login
  • Does NOT collect your name, email, or phone number
  • Does NOT track your location
  • Does NOT access your contacts or camera
  • Does NOT collect any personally identifiable information

Data We Access

The app only accesses:

  • Public Stock Market Data: We fetch publicly available stock prices and financial data from Yahoo Finance API
  • Internet Connection: Required to fetch real-time stock data

This data is:

  • Publicly available information
  • Not linked to any user
  • Not stored on our servers permanently
  • Cached temporarily for performance (cleared automatically)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • Yahoo Finance API: For fetching public stock market data. Their privacy policy: Yahoo Privacy Policy
  • Vercel (Backend Hosting): For hosting our analysis backend. Their privacy policy: Vercel Privacy Policy

Permissions

The app requires the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: To fetch stock market data from external APIs
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check if internet connection is available

We do NOT request any other permissions.

Data Storage

All stock analysis is performed on our backend servers. We temporarily cache analysis results for up to 24 hours to improve app performance. This cache contains only:

  • Stock symbols (e.g., "TCS.NS", "RELIANCE.NS")
  • Public financial data (prices, ratios, etc.)
  • Technical analysis scores

No user-specific data is ever stored.

Cookies and Tracking

We do NOT use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. We do NOT track your app usage, screen views, or any other activity.

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users aged 18 and above, as it deals with financial information and stock market analysis. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to secure. All communication between the app and our backend uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
  • You can uninstall the app at any time with no data concerns
  • No account deletion is needed (we don't have accounts)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Disclaimer

Important: This app provides stock analysis for informational purposes only. It is NOT financial advice. We are not responsible for any investment decisions made based on the information provided by this app. Always conduct your own research and consult with qualified financial advisors before making investment decisions.

Data Retention

Stock market data is cached on our servers for up to 24 hours for performance purposes. After 24 hours, cached data is automatically refreshed. We do not retain any user-specific data as we do not collect any.
